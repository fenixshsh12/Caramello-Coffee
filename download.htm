Starting from yesterday, July 23rd, 2023, Twitter, one of the largest social media platforms globally, underwent a major transformation by rebranding itself and adopting a new logo, now referred to as “X” This change was officially announced by Elon Musk, the owner of Twitter, declaring that Twitter is now known as X. Additionally, the domain name x.com now points to twitter.com, although there are concerns that the Twitter platform might undergo further changes or even cease to exist in the future.

Twitter or X vs Threads!
